About Us

This low-key hotel is 4 km from the Hizon-Singian House, dating from 1870, and 6 km from the Baroque San Guillermo Parish Church.

The understated rooms feature flat-screen TVs and free Wi-Fi, plus minifridges, and tea and coffeemakers. Room service is offered.

Complimentary perks include breakfast and parking. There's a casual international restaurant and a bar, as well as a lounge in the lobby.
